What is Edu:The Talent Scholar?

EDU:THE TALENT SCHOLAR is a scheme to provide value education to the children all over the world.This scheme is developed to give the value education to all students who are more addicted towards their career and seeking for new opportunities and the right key to unlock the door of their journey.

Why participate ?

  1. In this scheme we provide a valuable competition for all participating students.
  2. Here we provide the courses like EDUCATION,HEALTH,SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT and PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT on the demand of learners and according to the requirement of top companies all over the world.
  3. You will get the course in recorded video lecture format with properly running online classes, notes in pdf format with daily live doubt session classes.
  4. in live session classes/video lecture format, the associated teacher will teach you with the help of hand-made project with live experience.

Top performers will get:

  • The top position students will be awarded by prizes and the all students will get the a valuable internship certificate related to their selected chosen courses.
  • All the student will be awarded byDigital Certificate respective to their courses.
